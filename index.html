Isabelle

History

Since her first appearance in Animal Crossing:New Leaf, Isabelle has become a fan favorite in the Animal Crossing series.

Polite and hardworking, she is ever eager to assist the player in their role as town mayor. For her, the town she works in is like a family, she knows the ins and outs of management, and has enough influence to make a villager stop undesireable behavior.

Though not trained in martial arts, she was recently invited to participate in the Super Smash Brothers world tournament. Using her skills as an administrator, she has proven herself to be a capable fighter.
